1. APNs
- **What is APNs?**
    - Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) allows backend services to send notifications to iOS, iPadOS, watchOS, macOS, and tvOS devices.
- **Key Concepts:**
    - Device Token
    - Notification Payload
    - APNs Authentication (Certificates vs Tokens)
2. APNs HTTP/2 API
	- Apple Provider Authentication Token (JSON Web Token)
	- P8 Key vs P12 Key
