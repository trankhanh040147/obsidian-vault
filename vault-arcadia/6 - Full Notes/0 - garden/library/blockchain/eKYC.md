## KYC
- is a process by which financial institutions validate the identity of customers or businesses with whom they conduct business
- In India, all financial institutions except for regional rural banks are bound to follow the RBI KYC directions, 2016 and update the KYC data once in two years to once in ten years according to the risk associated with the customers
